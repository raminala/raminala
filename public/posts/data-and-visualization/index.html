Mass vs Weight

We will study mass vs weight relationship through a scatterplot

library(ggplot2)
ggplot(data=starwars, mapping=aes(x= height, y= mass))+
  geom_point()+
  labs(title="Mass vs. height of Starwars Characters", x= "Height (cm)", y= "Weight(kg)")
